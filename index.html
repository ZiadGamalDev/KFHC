ABOUT US

KFHC Ventures is a shariah-compliant venture capital firm that invests in early-stage technology companies in the MENA region.

It is managed by a team of experts at KFH Capital, the investment arm of Kuwait Finance House (KFH).

Its goal is to provide ‘smart capital’ to companies with a potential of high growth by giving them strategic support, expertise and access to the industry network.
